Enterprise-Grade AI Governance Compliance Framework structured specifically for an AI-enabled statutory audit platform operating under ISA, ISQM-1, ICAP Code of Ethics, Companies Act 2017, SECP, and regulatory oversight.

This framework is designed to be implemented inside AuditWise as a controlled governance module — not merely a policy document.

✅ AI GOVERNANCE COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK

For AI-Enabled Statutory Audit Systems

1️⃣ GOVERNANCE OBJECTIVE

Ensure AI usage:

Supports auditor judgment (does not replace it)

Complies with ISA 200 (professional skepticism)

Complies with ISQM-1 (quality management)

Maintains independence & ethics

Preserves audit evidence integrity

Prevents automated opinion issuance

Maintains full traceability

AI must assist — never conclude independently.

2️⃣ GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE
A. Oversight Roles
Role	Responsibility
Super Admin	AI infrastructure control (no data visibility)
Firm Admin	AI configuration & policy enforcement
Partner	Final approval authority
Manager	AI usage review control
Associate	AI drafting usage
B. AI Governance Committee (System Level)

Include configurable structure:

AI usage policy approval

Risk review of AI outputs

Periodic AI performance monitoring

AI bias & hallucination monitoring

Annual AI governance report

3️⃣ AI USAGE CONTROL MODEL
A. Mandatory Human Validation

AI-generated content must:

Be editable before approval

Require reviewer validation

Require partner final sign-off

Be blocked from direct report generation

No AI output may auto-approve or auto-lock engagement.

B. AI Tagging Structure

Every AI output must store:

Engagement ID

User ID

Timestamp

Prompt used

Model version

ISA reference linkage

Pre-edit content snapshot

Final edited version

Create ai_audit_log table:

ai_output_id

engagement_id

user_id

role

prompt_text

raw_output

edited_output

approval_status

timestamp

4️⃣ AI RISK CLASSIFICATION FRAMEWORK

Categorize AI usage into 3 levels:

🟢 Low Risk

Formatting
Grammar correction
Template generation

🟡 Medium Risk

Working paper narrative drafting
Risk explanation
Analytical commentary

🔴 High Risk

Materiality assessment assistance
Going concern commentary
Audit opinion assistance

High-risk AI output must:

Require mandatory manager review

Require partner confirmation

Display “AI-Assisted Content” disclosure flag

5️⃣ ISA COMPLIANCE SAFEGUARDS

AI system must enforce:

No AI-generated audit opinion allowed

No AI override of risk scoring

No AI modification post partner lock

No AI content without ISA reference tagging

AI narrative must link to evidence record

System must validate:

AI conclusion ≠ contradict documented evidence

If mismatch detected → trigger compliance alert.

6️⃣ ISQM-1 QUALITY CONTROL INTEGRATION

AI governance integrated into:

Monitoring & remediation process

Annual evaluation report

Engagement quality review (EQR)

Documentation retention policies

Generate:

AI Usage Summary per engagement
AI Risk Heatmap per firm
Annual AI Governance Report

7️⃣ DATA PRIVACY & SECURITY CONTROLS

AI must:

Not expose cross-firm data

Not retain data externally

Encrypt prompts in transit

Mask sensitive financial data if external API used

Allow firm-level AI API configuration

Implement:

Multi-tenant isolation

Role-based AI access

API key encryption

Activity logging

8️⃣ TRANSPARENCY & DISCLOSURE POLICY

System must:

Display AI-assisted indicator on working papers

Maintain full AI edit history

Allow export of AI usage report during QCR

Prevent deletion of AI history logs

9️⃣ PROHIBITED AI ACTIONS

AI shall not:

Issue audit opinion

Replace audit sampling judgment

Override materiality

Auto-complete engagement

Modify locked documents

Access other firm’s database

Violation attempt → block + log + alert.

🔟 MONITORING & PERIODIC REVIEW

Quarterly automated AI governance report must include:

AI usage frequency

High-risk AI content count

Override frequency

Post-edit modification ratio

AI detection of inconsistency

Partner override instances

Annual output:

AI Governance Compliance Certificate
ISQM Monitoring Summary
Remediation Plan (if required)
