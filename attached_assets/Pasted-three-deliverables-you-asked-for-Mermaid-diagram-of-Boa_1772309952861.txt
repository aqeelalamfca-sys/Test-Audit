2) 🧠 AI-Assisted Board Risk Scoring Integration (Architecture + Logic)
Objective

Add a Risk Score to the Finalization Board per user (and per engagement), so Partner/Manager can instantly see if the file is “safe to finalize” or “high risk”.

Risk Scoring Inputs (recommended)

Open items count (Execution)

Severity-weighted issues (High/Med/Low)

Evidence completeness (missing key support)

Unadjusted misstatements (ISA 450) materiality relevance

KAM presence (ISA 701) + whether properly drafted

Review backlog (items prepared but not reviewed)

Stale items (open > X days)

Mermaid — AI Risk Scoring Integration
Scoring Model (simple, explainable, audit-friendly)

Use a deterministic baseline (transparent) + optional AI narrative.

Example formula (0–100, higher = higher risk):

Open execution items: min(30, open_items * 2)

High severity issues: min(30, high * 10)

Medium severity: min(15, med * 3)

Evidence missing flags: min(15, missing_evidence * 5)

Unadjusted misstatements near materiality: +10

KAM required but draft incomplete: +10

Then AI adds:

Top drivers (why risk is high)

Recommended actions (what to close first)

Links (jump to filtered lists)

Output UI Recommendation

Add at top of Finalization board:

Risk Score: 72 (High)

Drivers:

3 High severity issues open (Execution)

2 unadjusted differences near materiality (ISA 450)

Evidence missing for revenue testing
